    Notes

    This site gives you piecemeal snippets to build your own form library. For that reason, there is no Download button anywhere. Maintain and optimize your CSS the way you need to; only use what’s needed.

    All <label> text are merely placeholders, and all <fieldset> tags are omitted. It’s advisable to use both in order to make your forms bullet-proof. Nested <label>s and <input>s are preferred, unless CSS demands siblings.
